Canaan (the Canaanite peoples)

The peoples of the land promised to Abraham — Canaanites, Amorites, Hittites, Hivites, Jebusites, and others — whose iniquity was 'not yet complete' in Abraham's day (Genesis 15:16). Their fortified cities fell to Joshua, but surviving enclaves (and their gods) ensnared Israel throughout the judges' era.

Historical Arc

Dispossessed in the conquest; remnants like the Jebusites of Jerusalem absorbed under David; Baal worship remained Israel's recurring temptation.

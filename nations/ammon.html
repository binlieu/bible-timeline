Ammon

The people east of the Jordan descended from Lot, centered at Rabbah. Jephthah fought them in the judges' era; their siege of Rabbah was the backdrop to David's sin with Bathsheba; Nehemiah's opponent Tobiah was an Ammonite official.

Historical Arc

Defeated by Jephthah and by David; oracles in Jeremiah 49:1–6, Ezekiel 25:1–7, and Amos 1:13–15.

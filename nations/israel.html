Israel (United Kingdom)

The covenant people descended from Jacob (renamed Israel), organized as twelve tribes, delivered from Egypt, and united as one kingdom under Saul, David, and Solomon with Jerusalem as capital from David's reign.

Historical Arc

Divided after Solomon's death (approx. 930 BC) into the northern kingdom of Israel and the southern kingdom of Judah.

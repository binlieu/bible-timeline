Judah (Southern Kingdom)

The southern kingdom under the unbroken Davidic dynasty at Jerusalem, comprising Judah and Benjamin. Its history alternates between reforming kings (Hezekiah, Josiah) and apostasy; it survived Assyria's invasion in 701 BC but fell to Babylon.

Historical Arc

Jerusalem and the temple destroyed by Nebuchadnezzar in 586 BC; the people exiled to Babylon; restored under Persia from 538 BC.

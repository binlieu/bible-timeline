Assyria

The Mesopotamian empire from Nineveh whose armies destroyed the northern kingdom (722 BC) and devastated Judah under Sennacherib (701 BC). Jonah's reluctant mission brought Nineveh a generation of repentance; Nahum later announced its doom.

Historical Arc

Nineveh fell to the Babylonians and Medes in 612 BC, as Nahum and Zephaniah had declared.

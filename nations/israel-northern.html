Israel (Northern Kingdom)

The ten northern tribes that broke from the house of David under Jeroboam I, with capitals at Shechem, Tirzah, and finally Samaria. Every northern king continued the calf shrines of Bethel and Dan; the dynasty of Omri and Ahab brought Baal worship to a crisis met by Elijah and Elisha.

Historical Arc

Conquered by Assyria; Samaria fell in 722 BC and the population was deported and resettled (2 Kings 17).

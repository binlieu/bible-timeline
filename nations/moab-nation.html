Moab

The people east of the Dead Sea descended from Lot. Balak of Moab hired Balaam to curse Israel; Ruth the Moabitess entered the line of David; David later made Moab tributary. The Mesha stele records Moab's ninth-century revolt against Israel.

Historical Arc

Made tributary by David; oracles of judgment in Isaiah 15–16, Jeremiah 48, and Amos 2:1–3; absorbed into later empires.

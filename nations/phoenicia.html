Phoenicia (Tyre and Sidon)

The maritime cities of the northern coast. Hiram of Tyre supplied cedar and craftsmen for David's palace and Solomon's temple; Jezebel of Sidon brought Baal worship into Israel; Elijah lodged with a Sidonian widow; Jesus ministered in the region and Paul landed at Tyre.

Historical Arc

Ezekiel 26–28 pronounced Tyre's fall: besieged by Nebuchadnezzar, and its island stronghold taken by Alexander's causeway in 332 BC (historical fulfillment noted by ancient historians).

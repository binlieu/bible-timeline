Rome

The empire ruling the biblical world of the New Testament: Pompey took Jerusalem in 63 BC, Herod ruled as client king, a census brought Joseph and Mary to Bethlehem, a Roman prefect crucified Jesus, Roman roads and peace carried the gospel, and Roman armies destroyed the temple in AD 70.

Historical Arc

Persecutor and unwitting servant of the gospel's spread; in Christian reading, the fourth kingdom of Daniel and the Babylon of Revelation's visions.

dominant conflict approx. 1200–960 BC

Philistia

The five-city league of the southern coast (Gaza, Ashkelon, Ashdod, Ekron, Gath), Israel's chief rival in the days of the judges and Saul: Samson's exploits, the ark's capture, and Goliath of Gath. David broke their power.

Historical Arc

Subdued by David; the cities declined under later empires and the prophets pronounced their end (Amos 1:6–8; Zephaniah 2:4–7).

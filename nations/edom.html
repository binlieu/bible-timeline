Edom

The nation descended from Esau, in the red mountains south of the Dead Sea. Edom refused Israel passage at the exodus, was garrisoned by David, and gloated over Jerusalem's fall — the burden of Obadiah's oracle. The Herodian dynasty of the New Testament was Idumean (Edomite) in origin.

Historical Arc

Judgment pronounced by Obadiah and Malachi 1:2–4; displaced into Idumea; the people fade from history after the Jewish wars.

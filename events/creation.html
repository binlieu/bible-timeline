Summary

God creates the heavens, the earth, and all living things, declaring creation good.

Genesis opens with God bringing order, light, life, and humanity into existence by his word. The account presents creation as purposeful, structured, and good.

Humanity is made in God's image and given responsibility over the created world. The seventh day is blessed as a day of rest, setting a rhythm that echoes through Scripture.

Historical Background

The account speaks into an ancient Near Eastern world but presents one sovereign Creator rather than competing deities.

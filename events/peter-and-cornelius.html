Historical Background

Archaeological evidence: Caesarea Maritima is well attested as the Roman administrative center of Judea, the same setting that produced the Pilate inscription. Scholarly opinion: Acts 10 is widely recognized as a decisive narrative for Gentile inclusion in Luke's theology. Historical reconstruction: A Roman centurion stationed at Caesarea fits the city's military and administrative role.

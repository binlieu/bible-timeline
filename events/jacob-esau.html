Summary

Jacob receives the birthright and blessing, continuing the covenant line through a troubled family story.

Jacob and Esau struggle from birth, and their rivalry shapes the next generation of the patriarchal family. Esau despises his birthright, and Jacob later receives Isaac's blessing through deception.

Despite human conflict and sin, the covenant promise continues through Jacob, who will later be named Israel.

Historical Background

Archaeological evidence: The massive Herodian stones and later destruction layers around Jerusalem confirm the scale of the temple complex Jesus discussed. Scholarly opinion: Interpreters differ on how much of the discourse refers to AD 70 and how much to final consummation. Historical reconstruction: Jesus' warning would have been heard against the visible grandeur of Herod's temple and later remembered after its destruction.

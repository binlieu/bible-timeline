Summary

Adam and Eve disobey God, bringing sin, death, and exile from Eden into human history.

The man and woman reject God's command and eat from the tree of the knowledge of good and evil. Their disobedience fractures fellowship with God, one another, and creation.

God judges the serpent, the woman, and the man, yet the passage also contains hope that the offspring of the woman will defeat the serpent.

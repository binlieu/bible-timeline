Theme

Law and Grace

The commandments given at Sinai as covenant charter, Israel's long failure to keep them, and the promise of a law written on the heart — the tension the New Testament resolves in 'grace and truth through Jesus Christ.'

Key Verses

  • Exodus 20:1–17 - The Ten Commandments
  • Deuteronomy 6:4–9 - The Shema: love the LORD with all your heart
  • Psalm 19:7 - 'The law of the LORD is perfect, reviving the soul'
  • Jeremiah 31:33 - The law written within
  • John 1:17 - Law through Moses; grace and truth through Jesus Christ
  • Romans 3:20–24 - Justified by grace as a gift

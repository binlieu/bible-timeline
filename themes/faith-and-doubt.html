Theme

Faith and Doubt

Abraham believing against hope, Israel doubting at Kadesh, Gideon's fleece, Elijah's despair, Peter sinking and denying, Thomas demanding to see — and faith counted as righteousness throughout.

Key Verses

  • Genesis 15:6
    - 'He believed the LORD, and he counted it to him as righteousness'
  • Numbers 14:11
    - 'How long will they not believe in me?' — Kadesh
  • Habakkuk 2:4
    - 'The righteous shall live by his faith'
  • Mark 9:24
    - 'I believe; help my unbelief!'
  • John 20:27–29
    - Thomas: 'blessed are those who have not seen and yet have believed'
  • Hebrews 11
    - The roll call of faith

Theme

Judgment and Mercy

The flood and the ark, Sodom and Lot's rescue, exile and return, Nineveh spared at Jonah's preaching: judgment announced, and mercy triumphing wherever there is repentance — 'in wrath remember mercy.'

Key Verses

  • Genesis 18:25 - 'Shall not the Judge of all the earth do what is just?'
  • Exodus 34:6–7 - Merciful and gracious, yet by no means clearing the guilty
  • Jonah 4:2 - Jonah's complaint: 'I knew that you are a gracious God'
  • Habakkuk 3:2 - 'In wrath remember mercy'
  • James 2:13 - Mercy triumphs over judgment

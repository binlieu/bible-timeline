Theme

Exile and Return

Banishment and homecoming as the Bible's recurring shape: Eden lost, Egypt and exodus, Babylon and the return under Cyrus — and the New Testament's claim that in Christ the true return from exile begins.

Key Verses

  • Genesis 3:23–24 - Driven from the garden — the first exile
  • Deuteronomy 30:1–5 - Scattering and regathering foretold
  • Psalm 137:1 - 'By the waters of Babylon... we wept'
  • Jeremiah 29:10–14 - Seventy years, then a future and a hope
  • Ezra 1:1–3 - Cyrus' decree: let him go up
  • Luke 15:11–24 - The prodigal's far country and the father's welcome

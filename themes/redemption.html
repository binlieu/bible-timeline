Theme

Redemption

God buys back what was lost: Israel out of Egypt by the blood of the lamb, Ruth by her kinsman-redeemer, the exiles out of Babylon, and — in the New Testament's proclamation — sinners by the blood of Christ.

Key Verses

  • Exodus 6:6 - 'I will redeem you with an outstretched arm'
  • Ruth 4:9–14 - Boaz the kinsman-redeemer
  • Isaiah 43:1 - 'I have redeemed you; I have called you by name'
  • Mark 10:45 - The Son of Man came to give his life as a ransom for many
  • Ephesians 1:7 - Redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of trespasses

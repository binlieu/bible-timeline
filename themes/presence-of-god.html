Theme

The Presence of God

God dwelling with his people: Eden, the burning bush, the pillar of cloud and fire, the tabernacle and temple filled with glory, the glory departing before the exile, the Word made flesh who 'tabernacled' among us, the Spirit at Pentecost, and the city where God is all in all.

Key Verses

  • Exodus 3:5 - Holy ground at the burning bush
  • Exodus 40:34–38 - The glory fills the tabernacle
  • 1 Kings 8:10–11 - The cloud fills Solomon's temple
  • Ezekiel 10:18–19; 43:1–5 - The glory departs — and returns
  • John 1:14 - The Word became flesh and dwelt among us
  • Revelation 21:3 - 'Behold, the dwelling place of God is with man'

Theme

The Remnant

God preserves a faithful few through every judgment: Noah's household, the seven thousand who never bowed to Baal, the survivors of exile who return, and the remnant chosen by grace of Paul's argument.

Key Verses

  • Genesis 7:23
    - Only Noah was left, and those with him in the ark
  • 1 Kings 19:18
    - 'Yet I will leave seven thousand in Israel'
  • Isaiah 10:20–22
    - A remnant will return — Shear-jashub
  • Ezra 9:8
    - 'A brief moment of favor... a remnant'
  • Romans 11:4–5
    - A remnant, chosen by grace

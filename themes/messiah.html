Theme

Messiah

The anointed one promised across the whole canon: the seed of the woman, the blessing of Abraham, the prophet like Moses, the son of David, the suffering servant, the ruler from Bethlehem — a hope the New Testament proclaims fulfilled in Jesus.

Key Verses

  • Genesis 3:15 - The seed of the woman — the first promise
  • Deuteronomy 18:15 - A prophet like Moses
  • Isaiah 9:6–7 - A child born, government on his shoulder, David's throne forever
  • Micah 5:2 - The ruler from Bethlehem
  • Matthew 16:16 - 'You are the Christ, the Son of the living God'
  • Luke 24:27 - 'Beginning with Moses and all the Prophets, he interpreted... the things concerning himself'

Theme

Covenant

God binds himself to his people by solemn promise: preservation to Noah, land and offspring and blessing to Abraham, law and belonging at Sinai, an everlasting throne to David, and the new covenant of the heart promised through Jeremiah and claimed at the Last Supper.

Key Verses

  • Genesis 9:8–17
    - The covenant with Noah and every living creature — the bow in the clouds
  • Genesis 15:17–21
    ; 17:1–8
    - The covenant with Abraham: land, offspring, blessing
  • Exodus 19:4–6
    - Sinai: a kingdom of priests and a holy nation
  • 2 Samuel 7:12–16
    - The covenant with David: a throne established forever
  • Jeremiah 31:31–34
    - The new covenant: law within, sins remembered no more
  • Luke 22:20
    - 'This cup is the new covenant in my blood'

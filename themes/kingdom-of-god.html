Theme

The Kingdom of God

God's reign asserted over all rival kingdoms: promised to David's line, dramatized in Daniel's visions of empires overthrown, announced by Jesus as 'at hand,' taught in parables, and consummated in the vision of Revelation.

Key Verses

  • Exodus 15:18 - 'The LORD will reign forever and ever' — the song at the sea
  • Psalm 2 - The LORD and his anointed against the raging nations
  • Daniel 2:44 - A kingdom that shall never be destroyed
  • Mark 1:15 - 'The kingdom of God is at hand; repent and believe'
  • Matthew 13:31–33 - Mustard seed and leaven — the kingdom's hidden growth
  • Revelation 11:15 - 'The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord'

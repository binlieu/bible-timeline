Theme

The Shepherd and the Flock

From the shepherd-king David and the LORD as shepherd of Psalm 23, through the failed shepherds of Ezekiel 34 and the promise that God himself will seek the lost, to Jesus the good shepherd who lays down his life for the sheep.

Key Verses

  • Psalm 23
    - 'The LORD is my shepherd'
  • Ezekiel 34:11–16, 23
    - God himself will shepherd; 'my servant David' set over them
  • Zechariah 13:7
    - 'Strike the shepherd, and the sheep will be scattered'
  • Luke 15:3–7
    - The shepherd who leaves the ninety-nine
  • John 10:11
    - 'I am the good shepherd'
  • 1 Peter 5:2–4
    - The chief Shepherd and the elders under him

Theme

Sacrifice and Atonement

Sin costs life, and God provides the substitute: the ram in Isaac's place, the Passover lamb, the Day of Atonement, the servant who bears the sin of many, and 'the Lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world.'

Key Verses

  • Genesis 22:13–14 - 'The LORD will provide' — the ram in Isaac's place
  • Exodus 12:13 - 'When I see the blood, I will pass over you'
  • Leviticus 16 - The Day of Atonement
  • Isaiah 53:5–6 - 'The LORD has laid on him the iniquity of us all'
  • John 1:29 - 'Behold, the Lamb of God'
  • Hebrews 9:22–28 - Christ offered once to bear the sins of many
